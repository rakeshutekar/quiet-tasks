# Quiet Tasks Privacy Policy

Quiet Tasks is designed to keep task data on your Mac.

## Data collection

Quiet Tasks does not collect, sell, track, or share personal data with the developer. The app does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs.

## Local task data

Tasks, subtasks, priorities, deadlines, recurrence settings, notification preferences, and widget state are stored locally on your Mac. The desktop app and widget share this data through the app's macOS App Group container.

## Google Tasks sync

Google Tasks sync is optional. If you connect a Google account, Quiet Tasks communicates directly with Google Tasks from your Mac. OAuth tokens are stored in macOS Keychain. Quiet Tasks does not send Google Tasks data or tokens to a developer-operated server.

## Notifications

Quiet Tasks can schedule local macOS notifications for deadlines and reminders. Notification settings are stored locally on your Mac.
